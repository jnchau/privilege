FAQ

Is this game real?
Privilege is mighty real, but this game not so much.
Why did we make it into a game?
To build empathy through role-playing in a digestible format. Also, to show the ridiculousness that these scenarios still exist and very real issues.
Can we make it into a real game?
Potentially! Let us know if you think it would drive the point better if it was real.
Why did you choose the ACLU?
The ACLU is one of the most well known and recognizable organizations that actively works to address civil right issues. Their work spans across many topics that align with our game.

Consider donating to help further their mission!
